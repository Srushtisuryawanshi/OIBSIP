The Captain Cool:M S Dhoni

Mahendra singh dhoni is an indian professional cricketer.He was the captain of indian cricket team.
He plays as a right-handed wicket-keeper-batsman and is known for his calm captaincy and his ability to finish matches in tight situations.
Dhoni is the first cricketer to be a winning captain of all three major icc trophies.(2007 ICC WORLD TWENTY20,2011 Cricket WORLD CUP, 2013 ICC Champions Trophy)
He is the captain of chennai super kings in IPL, leading them to final and winning in the 2010,2011,2018,2021 and 2023.
He is the 7th cricketer have scored more than 5000 runs in IPL
Govt. of india honoured him india's fourth civillian award padma shri in 2009 and third civillian award padma bhushan in 2018
He is leading brand endorser celebrity in India. The 2016 film, M S Dhoni:The Untold Story was made as a biopic on his life.
On 15 august 2020, Dhoni retired from all formats of international cricket and continues to play in the IPL.
